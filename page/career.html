About StockVins

StockVins is a tech startup specializing in digitalization and providing solutions for the wine industry. Began as an incubatee at Hong Kong Cyberport, StockVins launched Asia's first real-time fine wine exchange platform in 2019, allowing collectors to trade wines efficiently at low transaction costs, bypassing middlemen. At any one time, the tradable value of stock offered on our platform amounts to over HKD 500 million.

Entering 2021, StockVins will begin a fast-growing period to improve and expand our services while rapidly growing our business in the Greater Bay Area. We are looking for smart, talented, and dedicated people passionate about wine and technology innovation. Join us if you are up for a challenging and rewarding career and love working in an open and energetic environment!

Senior Graphic and Frontend Designer

Description

StockVins is looking for a talented and experienced designer who creates and delivers graphical designs and UI/UX designs. This role requires a blend of skills and responsibilities, including creative design skills, marketing sense, website strategy, UI designs, and knowledge in front-end development to assist in the development process.

You will have to communicate with our team members and work closely with our developers, including the development team in Shenzhen.

Your primary role and responsibilities:

  • Brainstorming, creating mood-boards and mockups, handling and managing any kinds of graphic designs.
  • Graphic design for App, websites, emails, and print materials.
  • Collaborate with content writers and developers to design web pages that tell a product story and help customers make purchase decision.
  • Participating in brand strategy and evaluation activities. Involve in UX testing and compatibility testings.
  • Create and deliver elegant web and app UI with user-centric design principles; and establish layout strategies, site architecture, and content hierarchy.
  • Wire-framing and designing high-fidelity UI designs, and creating interactive prototypes in Adobe XD. Preparing elements in optimized formats.
  • Developing layout designs in static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with best-practices.

Required Skills:

  • Minimum of 2 years of relevant work experiences.
  • Advanced graphic design skills with creative tools, including Adobe Suite, especially Illustrator, Photoshop, and XD.
  • Solid knowledge and experience in CSS.
  • Knowledge of web development protocols is required, including HTML, CSS, JQuery, HTML5 with JavaScript, and basic knowledge of PHP. You also need to have a board knowledge of the web development process.
  • Deep understanding of web structures, navigation flows, and responsive concepts in web design.
  • Understanding of web front-end architecture, e.g. React, and other related knowledge is an huge advantage.
  • Excellent global design sense and good understanding of the China market. E-commerce related experience is highly desired.
  • A team player, a fast learner and a proactive problem-solver; takes up challenges with aggressive deadlines.

This is a full-time position with a monthly salary range of HKD 22000-25000 depends on experience, with MPF and annual bonuses. We offer a flexible working schedule and environment.

The vision of StockVins is digitalizing the wine industry with technology innovation. The StockVins team is fast evolving, and we strive to build a culture that supports our growing and innovating environment. If you are ready to be a part of us, please apply today.

You MUST submit your portfolio along with your resume. Your portfolio should demonstrate strong skills in creative visual design and UI/UX design.
